Featured Work

Batangtoru LULC Change
Before–After Cyclone Senyar

Project Overview

Following the devastation of Cyclone Senyar — which triggered widespread flooding and landslides across Batangtoru, South Tapanuli — the regional government needed a clear, evidence-based picture of how the landscape had changed. The goal was to support disaster response planning and long-term land rehabilitation efforts.

My Approach

I conducted a multi-source supervised classification using machine learning on Sentinel-2 optical imagery, Sentinel-1 SAR data, and a Digital Elevation Model. The before-and-after analysis captured land cover transitions at the pixel level, distinguishing changes in forest cover, agricultural land, settlements, and bare ground caused by the cyclone's impact.
