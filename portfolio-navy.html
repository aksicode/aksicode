Featured Work

Flood Risk
Modeling Jakarta

Project Overview

The Jakarta provincial government needed a clear visualization of flood-prone areas to support urban planning decisions and community preparedness programs.

My Approach

Using SRTM Digital Elevation Model data combined with 10 years of historical rainfall records, I built a 5-class flood hazard zonation model covering all 44 sub-districts across DKI Jakarta's watershed areas.

What This Means for You

I can turn your raw geographic and statistical datasets into clear, decision-ready spatial analyses — delivered on time, with full documentation.
